The Veterans Health Administration (VHA), is the benchmark of excellence and value in health care and benefits. The Telehealth Management Platform continues this tradition of excellence by simplifying and standardizing telehealth business processes, including for scheduling appointments, telehealth administration, and managing resources.

The Telehealth Management Platform provides several tools that assist with telehealth operations.  Supported telehealth operations include:

  • Telehealth staff database (Providers, Preceptors, Telepresenters, etc.)
  • Resource Management
  • Dynamic Data Reporting
  • Credentialing & Privileging
  • Electronic Management of Telehealth Service Agreements
  • Scheduling Individual Appointments
    • Clinical Video Telehealth (CVT)
    • Store and Forward Telehealth (SFT)
    • VA Video Connect (VVC)
    • Telephone (PHONE)
  • Scheduling Group Scheduling
    • Clinical Video Telehealth (CVT) Group
    • VA Video Connect (VVC) Group
